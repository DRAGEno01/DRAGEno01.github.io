Year 7 & 8 Information Technology

OVERVIEW
Information Technology is a core subject in Years 7 & 8, enabling students to become equipped with the essential computer skills to use a range of software applications, which can be used in other disciplinary studies.

YEAR 7
Year 7 focuses on developing confidence in using a computer and builds skills in using a word processor, spreadsheet software and school learning management system and making short movies and websites.
During this course, students will develop touch-typing skills and learn how to manage files on the computer as well as on cloud storage.

YEAR 8
IT in Year 8 focuses on further developing confidence and learning new skills in various programs.
Students will further build on their understanding of the skills developed in Year 7 plus the introduction of programming.
